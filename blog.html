So after the huge success of animes like Konosuaba, a lot of animes started coming out in the comedy, isekai genre but then “Cid Kagenou” aka Lord Shadow made an entry into the world of anime which added something more into this isekai and comedy genre.

The Eminence in shadow (EIS) has broken the chain of the normal Isekai genre. The world mainly revolves around Cid Kagenou pulling the strings unknowingly. It starts as normal isekai with desires and dreams of the main character following his death and then being reincarnated in another world but things start to change when Cid rescued a girl and creates an elaborate plot about how he was the leader of an ancient organization, sworn to fight the Cult of Diabolos, to prevent the cult from resurrecting Diabolos. The girl swears her allegiance to Cid and so, he builds his organization, the Shadow Garden and their adventure begins.

This anime has a strong buildup plot giving the audience a chance to enjoy the entertaining journey of Cid Kagenou being immensely overpowered and trying to hide his identity to enjoy life as a viewer from the perspective of a background character. EIS has introduced a strange yet promising taste in reincarnation with an elite touch of sarcasm.

The only issue about EIS is the pace of the story which seems to be quite speedy and the background information and character development of side characters seem insufficient. Taking an overall stance The Eminence in Shadow gives a wholesome entertainment experience of a new kind of World dominating Isekai anime. Have a look at Lord Shadow’s immense power.
